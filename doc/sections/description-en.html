ERA Ontology. Version 3.1.0 (Working document): Description back to ToC

This is the human and machine readable Ontology governed by the European Union Agency for Railways (https://www.era.europa.eu/). It represents the concepts and relationships linked to the sectorial legal framework and the use cases under the Agency's remit, as described in the Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) [to be updated after publication] on the common specifications for the register of railway infrastructure [to be updated after publication].
